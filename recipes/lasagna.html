Lasagna

The following is a recipe for Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish originating from Italy. It may vegetables, cheeses and meats.

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • San Marzano Tomatos
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Prepare the tomatos in a food processor
  2. Cook the ground beef
  3. Add the tomatos and the cheese together and bake for 1 hour
